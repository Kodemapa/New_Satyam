Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common queries about admissions, academics, facilities, and campus life at Satyam International School.

Admissions Process

Our admission process follows four simple steps: 1. Online or Offline Registration inquiry. 2. Campus Visit & Counseling session with parents. 3. Application Submission along with required documents. 4. Student Interaction/Assessment (depending on the grade).

As per government norms, the child must be 3+ years of age as of March 31st of the academic year for admission into Nursery.

Mid-session admissions are considered subject to vacancy in the respective grade and the student's performance in the entrance assessment, especially for transfer cases.

Academics & Curriculum

Satyam International School is affiliated with the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi, up to Senior Secondary level.

Yes, we offer specialized integrated coaching for JEE (Mains & Advanced) and NEET for Grade 11 and 12 science stream students within school hours, led by experienced faculty.

We offer Science (PCM/PCB with optional CS/PE) and Commerce streams. We also provide NCC as an elective option for interested students.

Facilities & Campus Life

Yes, we have a fleet of well-maintained buses covering major routes across Ballari city and nearby surrounding areas. All buses are equipped with GPS tracking and female attendants.

Our campus boasts extensive sports facilities including a large playground for cricket and football, courts for basketball and volleyball, nets for cricket practice, and indoor facilities for table tennis, chess, and carrom.

Student safety is paramount. The entire campus is under 24/7 CCTV surveillance. We have trained security personnel at all gates, and entry for visitors is strictly regulated. Background checks are conducted for all staff.
