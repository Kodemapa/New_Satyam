Process of Admission
The admission process at Satyam International School, Ballari involves filling out an application form, submitting required documents, attending an interaction session (for certain grades), and completing the registration process upon selection.
For admission to Satyam International School, Ballari, you need to submit: birth certificate, previous school records/transfer certificate, report cards, Aadhar card, passport-size photographs, and medical records/immunization certificates.
Satyam International School, Ballari follows the age criteria as per board guidelines. Typically, children should be 3+ years for Pre-Primary, 5+ years for Class 1, and so on. Please contact our admission office for specific requirements.
Satyam International School, Ballari typically begins the admission process in January/February for the upcoming academic year starting in June/April. We also consider mid-year admissions based on seat availability.
For certain grades, Satyam International School, Ballari may conduct an age-appropriate assessment or interaction to understand the child's learning level. This helps us provide appropriate academic support from day one.
